The case takes place on Colorado, foot of the Rocky Mountains, located in a small town called Mizzurna Falls (a town with less than 2000 population).

Cathy and Emma Rowland attend at the same high school, since last night Emma dissapeared, her mother Sarah Rowland had tried to search her.
